Challenges

Struggles with clear communication

The company knew their work was important, and their clients agreed. However, the details of what they did and how they did it were difficult to communicate. They needed to present themselves professionally while appealing to both businesses and individuals, two very different audiences.

Approach

Finding the right tone

We focused on creating clear, concise content by reviewing all existing documentation and marketing materials. We restructured the narrative to create distinct differentiation between service offerings. Thorough research and mood boarding helped us explore visual directions before settling on a final look and feel that worked for both target audiences.
